Michael Smith

I'm a Computer Science + Learning Sciences PhD student at Northwestern University where I research as part of the Technological Innovations for Inclusive Learning and Teaching Lab (tiilt). Some of my research and project interests include exploring the intersections of technology & education, formal and informal learning, computing culture, new media and community, and games.
